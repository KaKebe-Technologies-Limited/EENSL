Magnetic particle testing

ZC-9D
AC/HWDC Electromagnetic Yoke

The light weight electromagnetic yoke is the ideal tool for detection of surface or sub-surface cracks and flaws in any ferromagnetic material. No other low cost, portable equipment offers such versatility in Magnetic Particle Inspection.

Fast and simple to operate, it is widely used with either dry or wet method fluorescent or non – fluorescent magnetic powders, in the testing of welds, automotive or railroad components, machine parts, steel and grey iron castings, forgings and in plan inspection, maintenance and repair.

ZC-9
AC Electromagnetic Yoke

AC electromagnetic yoke, ergonomically designed to improve productivity and reduce operator arm and wrist fatigue. The yoke generates a strong AC magnetic field for the detection of surface indications during magnetic particle testing.

Lightweight with an ergonomic grip and trigger, the ZC-9 is designed for the rugged demands of field inspection. Featurin a rugged cord and an isolated trigger switch; the ZC-9 is easily serviceable.

ZChek F9
Fluorescent Magnetic Particles

Fluorescent magnetic particles specially designed for forging and auto component application.

  • Fluoresces bright green – Brilliant Indication
  • Low Background – Enhances visibility
  • Encapsulation Technology – No loose pigment
ZChek F9W
Wet Method Fluorescent Magnetic Particles

ZChek F9W is the blend of fluorescent powder F9 and wetting agent WA. F9W is readily usable with water. The anti corrosion agents in WA prevent flash rusting of the components.

ZChek F18
Wet Method Fluorescent Magnetic Particles

Zchek F18 is high sensitive fluorescent magnetic particles designed to detect ultra fine defects. F18, thanks to its very low particles size offers high inspection reliablity.

  • Fluoresces Yellow Green – Optimum Indication
  • Very low background – Enhances Inspection Reliability
ZChek F18W
Water based Fluorescent Magnetic Particle

Zchek F18W is the blend of fluorescent powder F18 and wetting agent WA. F18 is readily usable with water. The anti corrosion agents in WA prevent flash rusting of the components.

ZChek F18W is a great choice for baths which need to be changed out frequently due to drag-in or heavy-use and is ideal for in-process testing to detect cracks, seams, inclusions, laps, tears and flakes.

ZChek F18W meets all major industry and NDT specification requirements, including automotive and ASTM standards.

ZChek FB
Oil-Based Fluorescent Magnetic Particle Suspension

Zchek FB is a fine blend of fluorescent magnetic powders suspended in ZChek 801 cart oil in aerosol can.

Zchek FB is an ideal choice for high performance inspections of precision safety-critical or high-stress components, and to extend the life of valuable mag particle equipment.

ZChek 9E Black
Oil-Based Visible Magnetic Particle Suspension

Zchek 9E Black is black magnetic powder concentrate designed for usage in non florescent wet method and on light colored part surface.

Zchek 9E Black is often used with Zchek WP white contrast paint to improve contrast and inspection sensitivity.

ZChek 9 Black
Oil-Based Visible Magnetic Particle Suspension

Zchek 9 Black is black magnetic powder concentrate designed for usage in non florescent wet method and on light colored part surface.

Zchek 9 Black is often used with Zchek WP white contrast paint to improve contrast and inspection sensitivity.

ZChek NF Red
Colored Non-fluorescent Magnetic Particles

NF Red is red magnetic powder concentrate designed for usage in non fluorescent wet method preferred for good contrast on gray, black or dark surfaces.

NF Red provides strong contrast on most metal surfaces during dry method mag particle testing in visible light. It is ready-to-use for visible light flaw detection of surface and slightly subsurface discontinuities in ferrous metals.

ZChek WP
White Contrast Paint

This is a blend of solvent and fine inorganic pigments. When sprayed, it provides bright background for magnetic powders, enhancing visibility of indications.

ZChek WP can be used with all types of colored and visible mag particles, including 9E Black or 9 Black wet method particles, and NF Red or 9D Red dry method magnetic powders.

ZChek 9D Red
Colored Non-fluorescent Magnetic Particles

General purpose magnetic powder formulated to form excellent indications of discontinuities at the surface or near surface of the test part.

ZChek Z 801
Carrier oil/ Cart oil

Cart oil is specially formulated carrier fluid for magnetic particle

Z-801 is made with highly refined petroleum oil with virtually no odor for improved operator comfort, while the high flash point and low toxicity reduce EHS concerns.

  • High Flash point – Safe
  • Low odor – Operator Friendly
  • No Background Fluorescence – More Inspection Reliability
ZChek WA-9
Water Conditioner

ZChek WA-9 is a general-purpose water-conditioning powder to enhance surface wetting and magnetic particle dispersion stability in water-based magnetic particle suspensions. Its unique blend of wetting agents, corrosion inhibitors and anti-forming agents eliminates the need for additional water conditioning agents.
